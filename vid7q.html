what will you improve/innovate/upgrade from the system developed?


In the field of wildlife conservation, the TrailGuard method is a great way to stop poaching, but it could still use improvements. The ability of the AI algorithm to tell the difference between people and animals based on body shape, facial geometry, and movement is impressive, but more fine-tuning could make it more accurate and reduce false alarms. Reducing the reaction time to under two minutes is important, especially when poaching happens at night. However, trying to find poachers in real time would be a game-changer. If it was used in places other than Africa with similar problems, it would help protect big mammals all over the world and help stop the sixth mass extinction, which is coming up soon.

NotCo's new way of making plant-based options has a lot of growth potential in the field of sustainable food production. Using AI to make more kinds of food than just Not Milk, Not Ice Cream, and Not Meat could encourage people to eat in a more sustainable way. It's also important that these replacements not only taste and feel like the original foods, but also have the same nutrient value. For example, putting a lot of thought into the nutritional value of Not Tuna can help protect the ocean and improve human health. Chef Sergio Barroso's dedication to taste is admirable, and it should continue to be the main focus of his work in the future.

In the case of predicting earthquakes, there are chances to improve and expand AI systems. Integration of real-time data from a bigger network of sensors can be used to improve the accuracy of predictions. New machine learning algorithms can help make more accurate guesses, especially about where and how big the next big earthquake will be. Also, ongoing study into earthquakes in the lab and the use of weak signals to predict earthquakes shows promise. AI and geospatial technologies working together could help manage earthquake risks on a bigger scale in a more complete way.

Lastly, AI can be used better to solve large-scale conflicts and crises, like food shortages, by increasing and improving the database used for data analysis and modeling. AI systems should be made more scalable so they can be used over a bigger area. This will make conflict resolution and crisis management more effective. Also, combining AI with other technologies like remote sensing and geospatial analytics can help solve world problems in a more comprehensive way.
