How Far is Too Far? | The Age of A.I.


The video looks at the pros and cons of artificial intelligence also known as AI. It has interviews with experts in the field, like William and Mark Sagar, who talk about the chance of AI becoming sentient or even conscious. They also talk about how AI could be used for bad things, like making weapons that can fight themselves or controlling people's feelings.

Some important questions about the future of AI are brought up in the movie. How far do you think we should go with AI? What are the risks of making artificial intelligence that is smarter than humans? How can we make sure that artificial intelligence is used for good and not bad?

I think the movie does a good job of showing how AI can be seen from different points of view. "How far is too far?" is a question that can't be easily answered. But the movie is a good way to start talking about these important topics.
